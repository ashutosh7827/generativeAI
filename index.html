About Generative AI

Generative AI refers to artificial intelligence that can generate text, images, videos, and code using large-scale models trained on huge datasets. It is used in art, writing, design, Generative AI is a branch of artificial intelligence that creates new content—such as text, images, music, videos, or code—by learning patterns from large datasets. Unlike traditional AI, which focuses on analysis or prediction, Generative AI uses advanced models (like GPT, DALL·E, or Stable Diffusion) to produce original outputs based on a given prompt.
